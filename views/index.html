About Me


Hey there, I'm Glyciant! You probably know me from around Twitch, Reddit or Discord for some of the moderation work that I do. I have been the lead moderator of /r/Twitch for a few years now and am also the owner of its Discord server. Alongside moderating /r/Twitch, I am also a member of the @TwitchSupport Volunteer team - meaning that I sometimes take the time to help out other users who may be having some basic Twitch troubles.

More recently, I have taken on various coding and development projects - and am continuing to build up my portfolio. My most notable projects include this site which you are currently visiting, and also Purple+: a promotion platform for members of the /r/Twitch community. I am now working on more moderation tools for Reddit, Twitch and Discord, as well as ways to engage communities. For more information on my projects, scroll down a little further!
